		~How to Use the App~
1. Once you start running the Navy Travel Calculator in Android Studio, 
   select an aircraft model from the drop-down menu.
2. Insert the coordinates, Latitude and Longitude, of the Start Location.
3. Insert the coordinates, Latitude and Longitude, of the End Location.
4. After all the information is inserted, press the Calculate Button.
   Then, the distance, travel time, and the number of refuel times will appear
   at the bottom of the screen. If you, cannot see the refuel section,
   the screen is scrollable.
